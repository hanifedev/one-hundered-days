## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **System Design:** The process of defining the architecture, components, and interfaces of a system.
*   **Scalability:** The ability of a system to handle increased load by adding resources.
*   **Reliability:** The ability of a system to perform consistently under expected conditions.
*   **Availability:** The percentage of time a system is operational and accessible.
*   **System Architecture:** The high-level structure of a system and how components interact.
