## ğŸ“š Dictionary for Developers

*   **Message Queue:** A mechanism for asynchronous communication between services, storing messages until they're processed.
*   **Event-Driven Architecture:** An architecture where services communicate through events rather than direct calls.
*   **Message Broker:** A service that routes messages between producers and consumers.
*   **Publish-Subscribe (Pub/Sub):** A messaging pattern where publishers send messages to topics, and subscribers receive messages from topics they're subscribed to.
*   **Asynchronous Communication:** Communication where the sender doesn't wait for a response before continuing.
