## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **Database Relation:** A connection between two database tables (one-to-one, one-to-many, many-to-many).
*   **One-to-Many:** A relationship where one record in a table relates to many records in another table.
*   **Many-to-Many:** A relationship where many records in one table relate to many records in another table.
*   **Join Table:** An intermediate table used to implement many-to-many relationships.
*   **Eager Loading:** Loading related data automatically when querying an entity.
