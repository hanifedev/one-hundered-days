## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **Agile Methodology:** An iterative approach to software development that emphasizes flexibility and collaboration.
*   **Sprint:** A time-boxed period (usually 1-4 weeks) for completing a set of work items.
*   **User Story:** A description of a feature from the user's perspective, written in simple language.
*   **Story Points:** A unit of measure for estimating the effort required to complete work.
*   **Sprint Retrospective:** A meeting where teams reflect on what went well and what to improve.
