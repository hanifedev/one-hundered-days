## ğŸ“– Task Explanations

Interceptors are a powerful feature in NestJS that allow you to intercept method execution before and after it happens. They can transform responses, add extra logic, extend method behavior, or completely override method execution. Interceptors are useful for cross-cutting concerns like logging, caching, and response transformation.

Understanding interceptors gives you fine-grained control over request/response handling. They run after guards but before pipes, making them perfect for transforming data or adding metadata to responses.
