## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **SMS (Short Message Service):** Text messaging service for sending short messages to mobile phones.
*   **Push Notification:** A message sent from a server to a user's device, even when the app isn't open.
*   **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Security method requiring two forms of identification (password + SMS code).
*   **Notification Channel:** A method of delivering notifications (SMS, push, email).
*   **Real-Time Communication:** Instant delivery of messages to users.
