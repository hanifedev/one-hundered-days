Search engines like Elasticsearch provide powerful full-text search capabilities that traditional databases can't match efficiently. ## ğŸ“š Dictionary for Developers

*   **Search Engine:** A specialized database optimized for full-text search and complex queries.
*   **Elasticsearch:** A distributed search and analytics engine built on Apache Lucene.
*   **Indexing:** The process of adding documents to a search engine so they can be searched.
*   **Full-Text Search:** Searching through the entire content of documents, not just exact matches.
