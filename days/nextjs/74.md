## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **CDN (Content Delivery Network):** A network of servers that cache and deliver content from locations close to users.
*   **Cache Layer:** A level in the caching system (browser, CDN, application, database) where data is stored.
*   **Cache Invalidation:** The process of marking cached data as stale and requiring an update.
*   **Edge Caching:** Caching content at edge locations (close to users) for faster delivery.
*   **Cache Strategy:** A plan for what to cache, where to cache it, and when to invalidate it.
