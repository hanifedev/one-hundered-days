## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **Accessibility (a11y):** The practice of making applications usable by people with disabilities.
*   **WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines):** International standards for web accessibility.
*   **ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications):** Attributes that make web content more accessible to assistive technologies.
*   **Screen Reader:** Software that reads web content aloud for users with visual impairments.
*   **Inclusive Design:** Designing products that are accessible and usable by as many people as possible.
