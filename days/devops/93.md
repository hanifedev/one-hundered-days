## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **Compliance Automation:** Automating compliance checking and reporting to meet regulatory and industry requirements.
*   **Compliance Framework:** A set of standards and requirements (SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA) that organizations must meet.
*   **Automated Compliance Checking:** Running compliance checks automatically to verify infrastructure meets requirements.
*   **Compliance Report:** Documentation showing compliance status and evidence of meeting requirements.
*   **Policy as Code:** Defining compliance policies in code (Terraform, InSpec) for automated checking.
*   **Continuous Compliance:** The practice of continuously checking and maintaining compliance, rather than periodic audits.
