## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **State Management Architecture:** The overall structure and organization of state management in an application.
*   **Domain Separation:** Organizing state by business domain (authentication, e-commerce, user preferences) rather than by technical concerns.
*   **Production-Ready:** Code that is well-structured, performant, and ready for real-world use.
*   **State Complexity:** The level of complexity in managing state, including nested objects, arrays, and interdependent state values.
*   **Scalable State Management:** A state management solution that can grow with your application without becoming unmaintainable.
