## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Vector Embedding:** A numerical representation of text, images, or other data in a high-dimensional space.
*   **Vector Database:** A database specifically designed to store and query vector embeddings efficiently.
*   **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG):** An architecture that combines a retrieval system (like a vector database) with a generative model to produce more informed responses.
