## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Agentic Workflow:** A sequence of agent actions and decisions that accomplish a complex task, often involving multiple agents and steps.
*   **Workflow Orchestration:** The coordination and management of multiple steps in a workflow, including execution order, state management, and error handling.
*   **State Management:** The process of maintaining and updating the state (data, context) of a workflow as it progresses through multiple steps.
*   **Workflow Pattern:** A reusable design for structuring agent workflows (linear, parallel, conditional, loops).
