## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Research Agent:** An AI agent specialized for conducting research, gathering information, and synthesizing findings.
*   **Information Synthesis:** The process of combining information from multiple sources into a coherent, comprehensive report or analysis.
*   **Source Citation:** Providing references to the sources of information used by an agent, enabling verification and transparency.
*   **Fact-Checking:** The process of verifying the accuracy of information gathered by an agent, often by cross-referencing multiple sources.
