## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Authentication:** Verifying the identity of users or systems accessing agent services (who you are).
*   **Authorization:** Determining what actions authenticated users or systems are allowed to perform (what you can do).
*   **Multi-Tenancy:** Supporting multiple isolated tenants (organizations, users) in a single agent system.
*   **Audit Logging:** Recording all agent actions and access for compliance, security, and debugging purposes.
