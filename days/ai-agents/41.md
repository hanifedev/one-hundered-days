## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Customer Support Agent:** An AI agent specialized for handling customer inquiries, support tickets, and customer service tasks.
*   **Knowledge Base Integration:** Connecting agents to knowledge bases (documentation, FAQs) to provide accurate, up-to-date information.
