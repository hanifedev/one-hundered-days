## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Cost Management:** The practice of monitoring, analyzing, and optimizing the costs of running agent systems.
*   **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO):** The complete cost of running agent systems, including API costs, infrastructure, development, and maintenance.
*   **ROI (Return on Investment):** The business value generated by agent systems compared to their cost, measured through productivity, savings, or revenue.
*   **Cost Optimization:** Strategies and techniques for reducing agent costs while maintaining or improving performance and quality.
