## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Swarm Intelligence:** A collective behavior that emerges from the interaction of multiple simple agents following local rules.
*   **Decentralized Coordination:** A coordination method where agents interact directly with each other without a central authority.
*   **Emergent Behavior:** Complex system behavior that arises from simple interactions between individual agents.
*   **Consensus Algorithm:** A method for agents in a swarm to reach agreement on a decision without central coordination.
