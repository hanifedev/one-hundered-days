## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Error Classification:** Categorizing different types of errors (LLM, tool, logic, user) to apply appropriate handling strategies.
*   **Recovery Strategy:** A plan for handling errors, including retries, fallbacks, escalation, or graceful degradation.
*   **Graceful Degradation:** Continuing to provide reduced functionality when errors occur, rather than complete failure.
*   **Error Prevention:** Techniques for avoiding errors before they occur, such as validation, checks, and confidence thresholds.
