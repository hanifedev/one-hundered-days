## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Self-Improving Agent:** An agent that can learn from experience and continuously optimize its performance, strategies, or prompts.
*   **Meta-Learning:** Learning how to learn, enabling agents to adapt their learning strategies based on experience.
*   **Feedback Loop:** A mechanism where agents receive feedback (from users, outcomes, or metrics) and use it to improve future performance.
*   **Continuous Optimization:** The ongoing process of improving agent performance through iterative refinement based on feedback and metrics.
