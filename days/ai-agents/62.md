*   **Healthcare Agent:** An AI agent designed for healthcare applications, requiring special attention to safety, accuracy, and regulatory compliance.
*   **HIPAA Compliance:** Meeting Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act requirements for protecting patient health information.
*   **Clinical Validation:** The process of validating that healthcare agents meet accuracy and safety standards for clinical use.
*   **Medical Disclaimer:** Clear statements that healthcare agents provide information only and do not replace professional medical advice.
