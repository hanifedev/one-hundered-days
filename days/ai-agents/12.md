## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL):** A model that requires human interaction to complete its task, typically for validation, correction, or clarification.
*   **Approval Workflow:** A process where an action must be explicitly approved by a human user before it is executed.
*   **Clarification Question:** A question asked by an agent to resolve ambiguity in a user's request.
