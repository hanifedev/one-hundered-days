## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Agent Collaboration:** Multiple agents working together to achieve common or related goals through communication and coordination.
*   **Message Passing:** A communication method where agents send messages to each other to share information and coordinate actions.
*   **Shared Knowledge:** Information or knowledge that is accessible to multiple agents, enabling them to learn from each other.
*   **Consensus Building:** The process of agents reaching agreement on decisions or actions through communication and negotiation.
