## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Educational Agent:** An AI agent designed to support learning and education, following pedagogical principles and privacy requirements.
*   **Pedagogical Design:** Designing agents based on educational theory and learning science to effectively support student learning.
*   **COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):** US regulation protecting children's privacy online, relevant for educational agents.
*   **FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act):** US regulation protecting student educational records and privacy.
*   **Adaptive Learning:** Personalizing instruction based on individual student needs, progress, and learning style.
