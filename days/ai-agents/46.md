## üí° Today's Dictionary

*   **Chain-of-Thought Reasoning:** A reasoning technique where an agent breaks down a problem into a series of intermediate reasoning steps.
*   **Tree-of-Thoughts:** A reasoning technique where an agent explores multiple reasoning paths (branches) to find the best solution.
*   **Planning Algorithm:** An algorithm that determines a sequence of actions to achieve a goal, considering constraints and dependencies.
*   **Meta-Reasoning:** The ability of an agent to reason about its own reasoning process, enabling self-improvement and adaptation.
